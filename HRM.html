Human Resource Management Course

Course Description

This course will teach you how to effectively manage human resources in your organization. You will learn about employee recruitment, selection, training and development, performance management, compensation and benefits, and employee relations. You will also gain an understanding of the legal and ethical issues that impact human resource management.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the importance of human resource management in organizations
  • Learn the key functions of human resource management
  • Develop skills in recruitment, selection, training and development, performance management, compensation and benefits, and employee relations
  • Understand the legal and ethical issues that impact human resource management
  • Apply best practices in human resource management to your own organization

Course Duration

The course will be taught over a period of 10 weeks, with one 2-hour class per week.

Course Fee

The course fee is $1000.
