Public Administration Course

Course Description

This course will introduce you to the field of public administration and its role in governing institutions. You will learn about the principles of public administration, including planning, organizing, leading, and controlling, as well as the different levels of government and their functions. You will also explore issues related to ethics, public policy, and citizen engagement.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the role and importance of public administration in governing institutions
  • Learn about the principles of public administration, including planning, organizing, leading, and controlling
  • Develop skills in analyzing and evaluating public policy and its impact on society
  • Understand the different levels of government and their functions in the administration of public policy
  • Explore issues related to ethics, transparency, accountability, and citizen engagement in public administration

Course Duration

The course will be taught over a period of 10 weeks, with one 2-hour class per week.

Course Fee

The course fee is $1000.
