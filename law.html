Law

Course Description

This course will provide an introduction to the fundamentals of law, including the sources of law, the structure of the court system, and the role of judges, lawyers, and juries. Students will also learn about various areas of law, including criminal law, tort law, and contract law, and will gain an understanding of legal reasoning and analysis.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the fundamentals of law, including the sources of law and the structure of the court system
  • Learn about various areas of law, including criminal law, tort law, and contract law
  • Develop skills in legal reasoning and analysis
  • Understand the role of judges, lawyers, and juries in the legal system
  • Apply legal principles to real-world scenarios

Course Duration

The course will be taught over a period of 12 weeks, with one 2-hour class per week.

Course Fee

The course fee is $1500.
